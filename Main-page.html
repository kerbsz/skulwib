Jumpstart Your Future at San Jose Litex Senior High School Program

As one of the best institutions for senior high in the Philippines, San Jose Litex Senior High School prepares students for their chosen program and journey through college. It offers the Academic Track which includes the strands Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), General Academic Strand (GAS), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), and Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM), as well as the Arts and Dessign Track.
